Welcome to the Hunar Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering the youth of Pakistan through quality technical and vocational training. Our mission is to bridge the skills gap and create opportunities for sustainable employment by providing practical and industry-relevant education.

Technical and Vocational Training:

The Hunar Foundation provides technical and vocational training to young individuals in Pakistan. The training programs are designed to equip students with practical skills in various trades, including plumbing, electrical work, welding, and more.

Skill Development for Employment:

The foundation focuses on skill development with the goal of enhancing the employability of its graduates. By offering hands-on training in market-relevant skills, the Hunar Foundation aims to prepare students for successful entry into the workforce.

Empowering Youth:

The organization is dedicated to empowering youth by providing them with opportunities for personal and professional growth. Through education and skill-building initiatives, the Hunar Foundation seeks to contribute to the socioeconomic development of individuals and communities.

Placement Assistance and Career Guidance:

The Hunar Foundation often provides placement assistance and career guidance to its graduates. This includes facilitating connections with potential employers, organizing job fairs, and offering counseling services to help students make informed decisions about their career paths.

Community Engagement and Outreach:

Beyond formal education and training, the Hunar Foundation may engage in community outreach initiatives. This could involve collaborating with local communities, conducting awareness programs, and participating in community development projects to address broader social issues and promote the importance of vocational education.

THE HUNAR FOUNDATION

The Hunar Foundation (THF) is a Not-for-Profit Organization (NPO), established in 2008 by a group of like-minded Pakistanis, to empower and enable the marginalized youth of Pakistan with vocational and technical skills. THF’s objective is to act as a catalyst in the field of education for catalytic action and increase the number of youth engaged in productive work, consequently alleviating poverty and making them contributing members of the society.
